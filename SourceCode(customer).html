FAQ

What are the pick-up times for the vehicles Are there any extra fees
Our office is open 24/7. When reserving a car, you can choose the pick-up time. During the inspection, any damages to the car may result in an additional charge on top of the rental period.
 
How long in advance do I need to book a vehicle? What are the payment procedures?
If cars are available, you can book and use the vehicle on the spot. After our mandatory inspection, you can make full payment for the car rental and damages (if any).
